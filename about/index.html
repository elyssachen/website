Elyssa Yiyan
About
     Elyssa is a graphic/fashion designer based in Naarm/Melbourne. She combines curiosity with tactility, specialising in creative direction, publication design, and (personal) identity.
    Her interests lay on a spectrum spanning from traditional techniques, like knitting, to new media, like creative coding. She is on the constant search from the ends of the forest to the depths of the world wide web.

Gallery designed and coded by Elyssa. Resume available upon request.
